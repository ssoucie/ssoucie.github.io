About Me

I'm a software engineer currently working at ION Group, a leading international fintech company. I graduated from Indiana University Bloomington with a BS in Computer Science with Highest Distinction. My specialization was Programming Languages, and I am grateful to have learned from some of the best minds in the field at IU. I was also a teaching assistant for CSCI-C 211, IU's introductory computer science course.

I was born and raised in Valparaiso, Indiana, a suburb of Chicago. I now currently reside in Chicago.

When I'm not programming, I'm probably watching or tweeting about IU sports (go Hoosiers!) or hanging out with my friends.

Interests

My main areas of interest are functional programming, mathematical logic, and philosophy (specifically philosophy of logic and philosophy of mind). I have a growing interest in logic/relational programming.
